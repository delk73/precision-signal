ST-LINK/VCP identity notes:
Flash commands pin ST-LINK identity with STFLASH_SERIAL to avoid default-probe
selection when both boards are attached.
Removing the USB extender materially improved ST-LINK stability. Direct
st-flash commands are diagnostic-only; the standard flash path is:
STFLASH_SERIAL=<serial> FW_FEATURES="<features>" make flash-ur.
Reset ordering:
Manual reset sequencing was unreliable. st-flash reset was also unreliable as an
orchestration primitive. The better no-button workflow was to leave Board B
running observer firmware, start the Board B capture listener, then flash Board A
actor firmware with make flash-ur so the post-flash reset starts the trigger/ack
sequence while the observer listener is already active.
