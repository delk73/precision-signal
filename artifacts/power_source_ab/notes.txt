Failure boundary

- last confirmed PASS: ~1.75 V measured rail
- first confirmed FAIL: ~1.56 V measured rail
- coarse operating band: ~1.56–1.75 V

---

Interpretation

- deterministic replay surface remains bit-exact across a wide undervoltage range
- no execution-level divergence observed for this firmware/stimulus down to ~1.75 V
- failure manifests as loss of capture / unstable execution below ~1.56 V
- external voltage reduction is confirmed to propagate to MCU supply rail (3V3)

---

Constraints / caveats

- result applies only to this minimal firmware and stimulus
- workload is low complexity and timing-tolerant
- USB remains connected (possible residual bias not fully eliminated)
- PSU resolution is coarse (±0.05 V)

---

Summary

Minimal firmware demonstrates stable, replay-identical execution across a substantial undervoltage range, with a first observed failure band between ~1.56 V and ~1.75 V measured rail.

No replay divergence was observed prior to failure; correctness holds until execution collapses.
