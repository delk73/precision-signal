# Physical Characterization

Physical characterization is separate from release evidence.

Generated physical characterization evidence lives under
[artifacts/physical_characterization/](../../artifacts/physical_characterization/).
Release evidence remains under
[docs/verification/releases/](../verification/releases/).

Replay authority remains:

1. CSV capture
2. record
3. artifact
4. replay
5. ResultBlock
6. artifact hash

Power, scope, and video materials are bench provenance only. They do not
redefine replay authority, release readiness, or retained release evidence.
